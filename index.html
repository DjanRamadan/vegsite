Evil Ahh Vegetable Site

Giggle Shitter

Is a tomato based mutant that likes to hide out in abandonded buildings detached from society. He likes to get high off of spray can fumes, giggle and throw his shit at teens unfortunate enough to be exploring his so called "kingdom".

Carrot Corpse

Once a radiant root basking in sunlight, the Carrot Corpse now festers in the Underbeds of the Underworld—buried alive in cursed soil, doomed to rot forever without the mercy of full decay. Worms whisper secrets to him through his withered skin, and he listens, twitching. His hollow eyes leak beta-carotene tears as he gnaws on the bones of lost bunnies. Every crunch is a scream. Every bite is a prayer that goes unanswered. He knows only one truth now: nothing grows here except guilt.

PuThaiTo

A balding, lumpy potato that got banned from every local park. Greasy, shirtless, and always breathing way too hard, the PuThaiTo lurks in the produce section pretending to shop just to make uncomfortable comments. He reeks of cheap cologne, loneliness, and boiled starch.

Peppers of Agony & Despair

Banished to the Scorching Pits of the Ninth Spice Ring for crimes against culinary decency, these once-mild peppers now eternally writhe in a stew of molten vinegar and ghost pepper extract. Their screams echo through hell’s pantry, each one flavored with bitterness and regret.

Onion of Never Ending Misery

A cursed culinary abomination born from the collective sorrow of a thousand chopped onions. He roams dimly lit basements.
